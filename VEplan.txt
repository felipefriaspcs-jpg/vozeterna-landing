# Do not offer 1 TB per person early

For VozEterna, I would **not** offer 1 TB per user at launch.

That is too much liability.

A family memory app can get expensive quickly because people upload:

```txt
- long videos
- high-res photos
- voice recordings
- duplicate files
- random test uploads
```

A thousand users with 1 TB each would be:

```txt
1,000 TB = 1 PB
```

At Supabase‚Äôs listed overage rate of **$0.0213/GB**, 1 TB is roughly:

```txt
1,000 GB √ó $0.0213 = $21.30/month
```

That is only storage. It does **not** include egress/download traffic, compute, database, transformations, backups, support, payment fees, or your profit margin. Supabase lists Pro egress overage at **$0.09/GB**, which can become the surprise cost if users stream or download media often. ([Supabase][1])

So if you charged someone $10/month for 1 TB, you could lose money.
